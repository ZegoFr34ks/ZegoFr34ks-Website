Kirito

-SWORD ART ONLINE-

Kazuto Kirigaya, born as Kazuto Narusaka, is a fictional character and the protagonist of the Sword Art Online series of light novels written by Reki Kawahara. He is more commonly known by his portmanteau, Kirito, his player name in the eponymous video game which the novels are partially set in.
